Scaling Smart: How Small Studios Can Thrive in a Shifting Games Industry

In a recent article on PocketGamer.biz, Gennadii Potapov (CEO of General Arcade) and Andrei Morozov (CEO of Lunate Games) explore how small game studios can scale effectively amidst the current turbulence in the games industry. They emphasize the importance of adopting flexible models for global teams, fostering platforms for learning and growth, and offering full-cycle solutions without expanding recklessly. The authors also highlight the benefits of cross-studio collaboration as a strategic move in 2025.
