Immerse yourself in an adrenaline-filled free adventure. As agent Rico Rodriguez, your goal will be to find and kill your friend and mentor who disappeared on the island paradise of Panau&#8230; Here at Panau, you must cause maximum chaos on land, water and air in order to change the balance of power.

Using a combination of a unique grappling hook and a parachute, you can perform unique tricks. Over 1,000 square kilometers of rugged terrain with hundreds of weapons and vehicles, Just Cause 2 defies gravity and prejudice.

The original game was released in March 2010. General Arcade specialists spend two weeks working on a custom engine C++ engine to prepare a DRM-free version with GOG Galaxy integration.
