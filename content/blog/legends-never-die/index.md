Fire Falcom studio with help of General Arcade team present you the reissue of Blade of Darkness, one of the first souls-like/soulsborne games ever made. It is hardcore fantasy action-adventure with unique combat mechanics and role-playing elements – pick one of four characters to hack and slash through a gory quest to save the world from the forces of Chaos!
