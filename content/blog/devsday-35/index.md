<p><a href="https://store.steampowered.com/news/group/4145017/view/3700310527377680731">Steam Minimum Price Threshold Update</a></p>
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img alt="" class="wp-image-2627" height="212" loading="lazy" sizes="(max-width: 666px) 100vw, 666px" src="Minimum-Price-Thresholds-Steam.jpg" srcset="https://generalarcade.com/content/uploads/2023/08/Minimum-Price-Thresholds-Steam.jpg 666w, https://generalarcade.com/content/uploads/2023/08/Minimum-Price-Thresholds-Steam-300x95.jpg 300w" width="666"/></figure>
<p>Valve has adjusted the minimum price allowed in some currencies other than the US dollar to align with the recommended currency conversions. The $0.99 low remains the same, and the update brings the lows in a number of other currencies in line with this price. If the cost of your products is below this limit, then they will become unavailable in some countries, so it is better to update the settings.</p>
