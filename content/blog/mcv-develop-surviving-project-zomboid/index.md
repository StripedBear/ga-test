MCV/DEVELOP is a magazine that has been focusing on the business aspect of the video game industry since 1998. The cover of the latest May issue featured Project Zomboid, whose multiplayer we helped develop.

Of course, if you see the cover, then there is an article nearby. Written by Richie Shoemaker, there is a conversation with Chris Simpson, co-founder of The Indie Stone. What is the secret of Project Zomboid success? How should you get inspired and by what games?
