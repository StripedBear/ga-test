<p><a href="http://blog.pkh.me/p/33-deconstructing-be%CC%81zier-curves.html">Deconstructing bezier curves</a></p>
<p>Animators, programmers, designers, and other graphics professionals and enthusiasts often work with Bezier curves. They are popular, widely documented, and used almost everywhere. However, they are explained almost exclusively in 2 or 3 dimensions, which can be a source of confusion in various situations. The author tried to deconstruct them a little further in his article.</p>
