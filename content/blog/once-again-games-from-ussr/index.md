<p>The first game that we couldn‚Äôt miss in any way is Disco Elysium. This RPG was developed and released by the Estonian studio ZA/UM and was released in October 2019. <p>The second game on today‚Äôs list is Loop Hero, an incredible retro game from the Russian studio Four Quarters, which was released as recently as March 2021. <p>An unusual mix turned out in terms of genre as well: Loop Hero is a roguelike game that managed to organically accommodate various elements of RPG, collectible card game and even a city-planning simulator. <p>The developers also approached the role of the player in an original way. <p>Another game that should never be overlooked is Planet Alcatraz, released by 1C studio in 2006. The game was based on Dmitry Puchkov‚Äôs book ‚ÄúDungeon orderlies‚Äù, which, in turn, is fanfiction based on the cult shooter Quake from id Software.</p>

<p>In September 2003, Alien Shooter came out, an arcade action game with RPG elements. <p>The players were asked to take on the role of a space paratrooper, but the game itself basically boiled down to the destruction of hordes of monsters attacking him by all possible means. <p>Although the game is a two-dimensional arcade, the player can still directly interact with the material world around him, in particular, the player can move various objects (chairs, barrels, explode gas cylinders) and break monitor screens.
