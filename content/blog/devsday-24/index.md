<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img alt="" class="wp-image-2318" height="368" loading="lazy" sizes="(max-width: 690px) 100vw, 690px" src="report-sony-fails-to-appeal-austrian-loot-box-verdict.jpg" srcset="https://generalarcade.com/content/uploads/2023/05/report-sony-fails-to-appeal-austrian-loot-box-verdict.jpg 690w, https://generalarcade.com/content/uploads/2023/05/report-sony-fails-to-appeal-austrian-loot-box-verdict-300x160.jpg 300w" width="690"/></figure>
<p>PlayStation is required to refund certain FIFA Ultimate Team packs. Sony had until April 3 to file an appeal, but did not do so. Now the company is obliged to pay ~340 euros to gamers who have previously applied to the court.</p>
