Don’t miss the chance to [get Ironcast in Epic Games Store for free](https://www.epicgames.com/store/en-US/p/ironcast)! The promotion ends on 7/15/2021, so you better be fast enough! Otherwise, you might miss the best way to take control of a 7 meter tall walking vehicle called an Ironcast and fight against enemy Ironcasts to defend 1880’s Victorian England…

This inspired by Victorian-era science fiction that lets you to fight the fierce battles by generating resource nodes, which in turn drive the Ironcast’s various weapons and systems. Сhoose how to spend these nodes wisely, either offensively in order to cripple and destroy your opponents, or defensively, if they suspect a barrage of incoming weapons fire is due.

The company that worked on that release is General Arcade, and the technology used is [Ghost Porting](https://generalarcade.com/ghost-porting/). Ghost Porting is techniques and tools which let us to port different titles to different PC stores and prepare DRM-free releases without even getting an access to the source code, just by having binaries. And yes, that is verily unique!
