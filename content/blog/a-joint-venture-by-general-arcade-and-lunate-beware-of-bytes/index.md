<h6 id="h-we-re-teaming-up-with-lunate-games-to-launch-beware-of-bytes-a-strategic-joint-venture-built-for-scale-technical-excellence-and-stability-in-today-s-evolving-industry">We’re teaming up with <a href="https://www.linkedin.com/company/lunate-games/">Lunate Games</a> to launch <a href="https://bewareofbytes.com">Beware of Bytes!</a>, a strategic joint venture built for scale, technical excellence, and stability in today’s evolving industry.</h6>
<h5 id="h-what-this-means-for-the-games-industry"><a href="https://www.gamespress.com/en-GB/General-Arcade-and-Lunate-Games-join-forces-for-Beware-of-Bytes---Doub">What This Means for The Games Industry?</a></h5>
<h6 id="h-two-powerhouse-teams-are-now-one">Two powerhouse teams are now one</h6>
<p>We’re doubling our engineering strength and expanding our expertise across <a href="https://www.linkedin.com/company/unity/">Unity</a>, <a href="https://www.linkedin.com/company/unreal-engine-for-design-visualization/">Unreal Engine</a>, CryEngine, and custom engines. Whether it’s porting, co-dev, remastering, performance optimisation, tools development, or network implementation, our partnership delivers cutting-edge solutions at a whole new level!</p>
<h6 id="h-global-reach-local-expertise">Global reach, local expertise</h6>
<p>With HQs in Singapore and offices in Singapore, Malaysia, Finland, and Georgia, we operate seamlessly across time zones, ensuring speed, efficiency, and top-tier support for developers worldwide.</p>
<h6 id="h-built-for-big-games-designed-for-the-future">Built for big games, designed for the future</h6>
<p>Our combined experience includes acclaimed titles such as Baldur’s Gate 3, Dragon’s Dogma, Shadow Warrior, Crysis Remastered, Remnant: From the Ashes and Kingdom Come Deliverance.</p>
<h6 id="h-scaling-smart-securing-talent">Scaling smart, securing talent</h6>
<p>At a time when the industry is facing uncertainty, Beware of Bytes! is a model for scaling strategically, boosting capacity while prioritizing job security for our teams.</p>
<p>We couldn’t be more excited to push boundaries, tackle bigger projects, and build the future of game development together.</p>
<p><em>This is just the beginning — stay tuned!</em></p>
