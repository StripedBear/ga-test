<p><a href="https://www.cyanilux.com/tutorials/waterfall-shader-breakdown/">Waterfall shader analysis</a></p>
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img alt="" class="wp-image-2429" height="277" loading="lazy" sizes="(max-width: 326px) 100vw, 326px" src="waterfall-shader-breakdown.png" srcset="https://generalarcade.com/content/uploads/2023/06/waterfall-shader-breakdown.png 326w, https://generalarcade.com/content/uploads/2023/06/waterfall-shader-breakdown-300x255.png 300w" width="326"/></figure>
<p>Base the effect with an SDF (Signed Distance Field) sphere, but the Y position is fixed so that a hole is created underneath. To make it more liquid-like, this hole and the sides of the waterfall are distorted with a sine wave and clipped at the alpha channel. Noise is used to add foam to make it more like flowing water.</p>
