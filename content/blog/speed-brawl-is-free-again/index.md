London, 1888. After a decade of war with the Lunar invaders we named the Selenites, the British Empire finally vanquished the insect-like menace thanks to a dashing officer by the name of Hugo Wells. He crushed the colony’s queen and in doing so cemented his status as both a hero of the empire, and the de facto patriarch of the drone-like Selenites…

2D combat-racer Speed Brawl is absolutely free in Epic Games store once again! Make sure to [grab it on time](https://www.epicgames.com/store/en-US/p/speed-brawl), sale ends 9/23/2021 at 6:00 PM.

General Arcade [worked on Speed Brawl for eight months](https://generalarcade.com/project/speed-brawl/). Three programmers and two testers used Unity, C# and C++ to make cross-platform multiplayer for PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Nintendo Switch, Steam, GOG and EGS via Epic Online Services.
